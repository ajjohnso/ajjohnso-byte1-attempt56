Interactive Data Science Byte 1


Where have school shootings occurred in the United States from 2010-2017?


This interactive map shows where school shooting have taken place over the last 7 years. You can click on each marker and get a count of the deaths and injuries that happend at each incident. Other data I would have liked to include in this byte would be the urban vs rural distinction of school schooting, and the type of gun policies in each state.
